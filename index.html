Installation/usage guide

Drag the Growdice Helper button to your bookmark bar if you DO NOT HAVE a bookmark bar press CTRL+SHIFT+B on your keyboard. This will toggle the bookmarkbar. When you have the Growdice Helper in your bookmark bar go back to growdice. Once loaded into growdice press the Growdice Helper in your bookmark bar. Now the Growdice Helper should show up.
